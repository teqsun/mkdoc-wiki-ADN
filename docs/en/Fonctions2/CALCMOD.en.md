# Calcmod

## Calcmod

The ** Calcmod ** Function Creates A Constant Digital Variable corresponding to the Modulo of the Digure Indicated in Parameter.

The Modulo is the rest of the Whole Division.

### Syntax: & nbsp;

Q01.CALCMOD (10)

Gold

\ _Calcmod (Q01; 10)
