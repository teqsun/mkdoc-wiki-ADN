# Capitalize

## Capitalize Function

The **Capitalize** Function Modifies The Breakdown of Responses to a Literal Variable So that each word begins with a capital letter, followed by Lowcase

### Syntax: & nbsp;

Q01.capitalize ()

Gold

\ _Capitalize (Q01)

& nbsp;

Applies to a literal variable.

& nbsp;
