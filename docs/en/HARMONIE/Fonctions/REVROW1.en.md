# Revrow

## Revrow Function

The function ** Revrow ** Reverse the lines of the variable treated: the 1st element becomes the last, the 2nd the penultimate, etc.

### Syntax: & nbsp;

Q01.REVROW ()

Or

\ _REVROW (Q01)

& nbsp;
