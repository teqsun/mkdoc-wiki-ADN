# Titmod

## Titmod Function

The **Titmod** Function Applied To A Filter (A Variable to A Modality) and Reverses its Title and the Title of the Modality.

### Syntax: & nbsp;

Q01.titmod ()

Or

\ _Titmod (Q01)
