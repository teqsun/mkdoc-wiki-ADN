# Capitalize

## Capitalize Function

The ** Capitalize ** function modifies the breakdown of responses to a literal variable so that each word begins with a capital letter, followed by lowercase

### Syntax: & nbsp;

Q01.capitalize ()

Gold

\ _Capitalize (Q01)

& nbsp;

Applied to a variable literal.

& nbsp;
